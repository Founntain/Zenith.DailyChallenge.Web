Should the leaderboard be removed / replaced?

Currently there is a big discussion in regards of the current leaderboard. These reach from the removal to the leaderboard entirely or replacing it with a cycling leaderboard (weekly/monthly). Reason is the all time leaderboard can be discouraging for new players to even appear on said leaderboard or not being a reason to even play as it doesn't matter, compared to the people that are already here since a long time. In the end the Zenith Daily Challenge was made to give players an extra way to play Quick Play and also giving the players some more insights to their QP Stats.
So this brings the question: Should the all time leaderboard be removed?
There are multiple proposed solutions to the issue:
  • Remove it entirely
  • Replace it with a cycling leaderboard (weekly, monthly or whatever)
  • Have a cycling one and the all time leaderboard, while the cycling one would be the main one
  • Keep it as it is
Most of the stuff we discuss is done on the TETR.IO Discord Server in the #quick-play > Zenith Daily Challange thread or on GitHub.

I would love to hear your opinion to that topic and going forward with you people! There is a poll on the discord that runs until 25th August 2025
