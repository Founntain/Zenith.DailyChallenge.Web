Announcing a new way to enjoy QP2 challenges! ZDC - Challenge Hub working title
Create! Create your own challenges completely from scratch! Add as many conditions as you want! Mods? Yes, sure! Need to go fast and reach a specific Floor in a specific amount of time? I got you covered! Own Title and description is also possible maybe you want to give your challenge some lore?
Share! Share your challenges with others so they can play your creations. Sharing is as simple as pressing a button. Private challenges are also possible, only people with the link to the challenge can compete.
Compete! Compete on created challenges on their own leaderboard to see who is the best! You decide what the important stat is to sort the leaderboard by. Altitude High Flighers? Spin King? Speedrun maniac? You decide! Want to create a limited time challenge? Go ahead and set a time limit for your challenge! Competitors will only be able to compete within the specified time frame.
Go featured! Your challenge got popular? Great, there is a high chance you and your challenge will be featured on the main page! Featured challenges can award score! No worries, if you already played it before it got featured, you will still be able to gain score on it.
